# CREDIT AGREEMENT

**Date**: January 15, 2024

## PARTIES

**BORROWER:**  
Acme Corporation  
123 Business Street  
New York, NY 10001  
Tax ID: 12-3456789

**LENDERS:**  
1. First National Bank (Administrative Agent)  
2. Global Finance Partners LLC  
3. Capital Investment Group

## ARTICLE I - DEFINITIONS

**"Agreement"** means this Credit Agreement and all amendments, modifications, and supplements hereto.

**"Borrower"** means Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation.

**"Credit Facility"** means the revolving credit facility described in Article II.

**"SOFR"** means the Secured Overnight Financing Rate.

**"Maturity Date"** means January 15, 2029, being five (5) years from the Effective Date.

## ARTICLE II - FACILITY TERMS

### 2.1 Facility Amount
The Lenders agree to provide a revolving credit facility in the aggregate principal amount of **FIFTY MILLION DOLLARS ($50,000,000)**.

### 2.2 Interest Rate
The outstanding principal balance shall bear interest at a rate per annum equal to **SOFR plus 2.50%** (the "Applicable Margin").

### 2.3 Maturity
All outstanding principal and accrued interest shall be due and payable on the Maturity Date, unless earlier prepaid or accelerated.

### 2.4 Commitment Fee
The Borrower shall pay a commitment fee of **0.25%** per annum on the unused portion of the Credit Facility.

### 2.5 Arrangement Fee
An upfront arrangement fee of **$500,000** is payable on the Effective Date.

## ARTICLE III - FINANCIAL COVENANTS

The Borrower covenants and agrees to maintain at all times:

### 3.1 Debt to EBITDA Ratio
The ratio of Total Debt to EBITDA shall not exceed **3.5 to 1.0**, tested quarterly.

### 3.2 Interest Coverage Ratio
The ratio of EBITDA to Interest Expense shall not be less than **2.0 to 1.0**, tested quarterly.

### 3.3 Current Ratio
The ratio of Current Assets to Current Liabilities shall not be less than **1.5 to 1.0**, tested quarterly.

### 3.4 Minimum Liquidity
The Borrower shall maintain minimum unrestricted cash and cash equivalents of **$5,000,000** at all times.

## ARTICLE IV - REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

The Borrower represents and warrants that:

### 4.1 Organization and Authority
The Borrower is a corporation duly organized, validly existing, and in good standing under the laws of the State of Delaware.

### 4.2 Financial Condition
The financial statements previously delivered to the Lenders fairly present the financial condition of the Borrower as of their respective dates.

### 4.3 No Material Adverse Change
Since December 31, 2023, there has been no material adverse change in the business, operations, properties, or financial condition of the Borrower.

### 4.4 Litigation
There are no actions, suits, or proceedings pending or threatened against the Borrower that could reasonably be expected to have a material adverse effect.

### 4.5 Compliance with Laws
The Borrower is in compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and orders.

## ARTICLE V - CONDITIONS PRECEDENT

### 5.1 Initial Conditions
The obligation of the Lenders to make the initial advance is subject to:

a) Receipt of executed loan documents  
b) Payment of arrangement fee  
c) Delivery of corporate resolutions  
d) Receipt of legal opinions  
e) Evidence of insurance coverage

### 5.2 Subsequent Conditions
Each advance is subject to:

a) No default or event of default existing  
b) Representations and warranties being true  
c) Compliance with all covenants

## ARTICLE VI - PREPAYMENT

### 6.1 Voluntary Prepayment
The Borrower may prepay the Credit Facility in whole or in part at any time without premium or penalty, provided that:

a) At least three (3) business days' prior written notice is given  
b) Prepayments are in minimum amounts of $1,000,000  
c) Accrued interest is paid on the prepaid amount

### 6.2 Mandatory Prepayment
The Borrower shall prepay the Credit Facility upon:

a) Asset sales exceeding $10,000,000 in any fiscal year  
b) Issuance of equity securities  
c) Incurrence of additional indebtedness

## ARTICLE VII - REPORTING REQUIREMENTS

### 7.1 Annual Financial Statements
Within 90 days after each fiscal year end, audited financial statements.

### 7.2 Quarterly Financial Statements
Within 45 days after each fiscal quarter end, unaudited financial statements.

### 7.3 Compliance Certificate
With each financial statement delivery, a certificate of compliance with all covenants.

### 7.4 Notice of Default
Immediate notice of any default or event of default.

### 7.5 Material Events
Prompt notice of any material adverse change or litigation.

## ARTICLE VIII - AFFIRMATIVE COVENANTS

The Borrower covenants to:

### 8.1 Maintain Existence
Preserve and maintain its corporate existence and rights.

### 8.2 Maintain Insurance
Maintain insurance coverage as required by the Lenders.

### 8.3 Pay Obligations
Pay all taxes and other obligations when due.

### 8.4 Maintain Properties
Maintain all properties in good condition.

### 8.5 Comply with Laws
Comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

## ARTICLE IX - NEGATIVE COVENANTS

The Borrower shall not, without prior written consent:

### 9.1 Incur Additional Debt
Incur indebtedness exceeding $5,000,000 in aggregate.

### 9.2 Create Liens
Create or permit liens on assets, except permitted liens.

### 9.3 Make Acquisitions
Make acquisitions exceeding $10,000,000 in any fiscal year.

### 9.4 Pay Dividends
Pay dividends if a default exists or would result.

### 9.5 Change Business
Make any material change in the nature of the business.

## ARTICLE X - EVENTS OF DEFAULT

The following constitute Events of Default:

### 10.1 Payment Default
Failure to pay principal or interest within five (5) business days of due date.

### 10.2 Covenant Default
Failure to comply with any covenant, continuing for thirty (30) days after notice.

### 10.3 Representation Default
Any representation or warranty proves to be materially incorrect.

### 10.4 Cross-Default
Default under any other indebtedness exceeding $2,000,000.

### 10.5 Bankruptcy
Commencement of bankruptcy or insolvency proceedings.

### 10.6 Judgment Default
Entry of judgments exceeding $3,000,000 in aggregate, remaining unsatisfied for sixty (60) days.

### 10.7 Material Adverse Change
Occurrence of a material adverse change in the business or financial condition.

## ARTICLE XI - REMEDIES

Upon occurrence of an Event of Default:

### 11.1 Acceleration
The Lenders may declare all amounts immediately due and payable.

### 11.2 Enforcement
The Lenders may exercise all rights and remedies available at law or in equity.

### 11.3 Set-Off
The Lenders may set off any amounts owed to the Borrower.

## ARTICLE XII - SECURITY

### 12.1 Collateral
This Credit Facility is secured by:

a) First priority lien on all accounts receivable  
b) First priority lien on inventory  
c) Pledge of 100% of stock in subsidiaries

### 12.2 Guarantees
Personal guarantees from principal shareholders required.

## ARTICLE XIII - MISCELLANEOUS

### 13.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

### 13.2 Jurisdiction
The parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in New York County, New York.

### 13.3 Amendments
No amendment except in writing signed by all parties.

### 13.4 Notices
All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses set forth above.

### 13.5 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
