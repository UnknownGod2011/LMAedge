COMPREHENSIVE LOAN AGREEMENT

ARTICLE 1: PARTIES AND DEFINITIONS

This Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2024, between:

BORROWER: TechCorp Industries, Inc., a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 123 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105

LENDER: Global Finance Bank, N.A., a national banking association with principal offices at 456 Wall Street, New York, NY 10005

DEFINITIONS:
"Loan" means the term loan facility in the principal amount of $50,000,000
"Interest Rate" means SOFR plus 3.50% per annum
"Maturity Date" means January 15, 2029 (5 years from closing)
"Financial Covenants" means the covenants set forth in Article 7

ARTICLE 2: LOAN AMOUNT AND PURPOSE

2.1 Principal Amount: Lender agrees to provide Borrower with a term loan in the aggregate principal amount of Fifty Million Dollars ($50,000,000).

2.2 Purpose: The proceeds of the Loan shall be used exclusively for: (a) acquisition of manufacturing equipment ($30,000,000), (b) working capital needs ($15,000,000), and (c) refinancing existing debt ($5,000,000).

2.3 Single Disbursement: The entire Loan amount shall be disbursed in a single advance on the Closing Date.

ARTICLE 3: INTEREST RATE AND CALCULATION

3.1 Interest Rate: The Loan shall bear interest at a rate per annum equal to the Secured Overnight Financing Rate (SOFR) plus 3.50% (the "Interest Rate").

3.2 Default Rate: Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default, the Interest Rate shall increase by 2.00% per annum.

3.3 Interest Calculation: Interest shall be calculated on the basis of actual days elapsed and a 360-day year.

3.4 Interest Payment Dates: Interest shall be payable quarterly in arrears on March 31, June 30, September 30, and December 31 of each year.

ARTICLE 4: FEES AND CHARGES

4.1 Origination Fee: Borrower shall pay Lender an origination fee of 1.00% of the Loan amount ($500,000), payable on the Closing Date.

4.2 Commitment Fee: A commitment fee of 0.50% per annum on the unused portion of the commitment.

4.3 Late Payment Fee: Any payment not received within 5 business days of the due date shall incur a late fee of 5% of the overdue amount.

4.4 Prepayment Premium: Prepayments made within the first 24 months shall be subject to a premium of 2% of the amount prepaid.

ARTICLE 5: REPAYMENT SCHEDULE

5.1 Amortization: The Loan shall amortize in 20 equal quarterly installments of principal, commencing on March 31, 2024.

5.2 Quarterly Payment Amount: Each quarterly payment shall be $2,500,000 plus accrued interest.

5.3 Final Payment: The entire outstanding principal balance, together with all accrued and unpaid interest, shall be due and payable on the Maturity Date.

5.4 Payment Method: All payments shall be made by wire transfer to Lender's account specified in Schedule A.

ARTICLE 6: PREPAYMENT PROVISIONS

6.1 Voluntary Prepayment: Borrower may prepay the Loan in whole or in part at any time, subject to the prepayment premium set forth in Section 4.4.

6.2 Mandatory Prepayment: Borrower shall prepay the Loan upon: (a) sale of substantially all assets, (b) receipt of insurance proceeds exceeding $5,000,000, or (c) issuance of equity securities raising more than $10,000,000.

6.3 Application of Prepayments: Voluntary prepayments shall be applied to installments in inverse order of maturity.

ARTICLE 7: FINANCIAL COVENANTS

7.1 Debt Service Coverage Ratio: Borrower shall maintain a Debt Service Coverage Ratio of not less than 1.25:1.00, tested quarterly.

7.2 Leverage Ratio: Borrower shall maintain a Total Debt to EBITDA ratio of not more than 3.50:1.00, tested quarterly.

7.3 Current Ratio: Borrower shall maintain a Current Ratio of not less than 1.50:1.00, tested quarterly.

7.4 Minimum EBITDA: Borrower shall maintain minimum EBITDA of $15,000,000 per fiscal year.

7.5 Capital Expenditures: Borrower shall not make Capital Expenditures exceeding $10,000,000 in any fiscal year without Lender's prior written consent.

ARTICLE 8: AFFIRMATIVE COVENANTS

8.1 Financial Statements: Borrower shall deliver to Lender: (a) audited annual financial statements within 90 days of fiscal year end, (b) unaudited quarterly financial statements within 45 days of quarter end, and (c) monthly management reports within 20 days of month end.

8.2 Compliance Certificates: Together with each financial statement delivery, Borrower shall provide a Compliance Certificate certifying compliance with all covenants.

8.3 Insurance: Borrower shall maintain comprehensive insurance coverage including property, liability, and business interruption insurance with Lender named as loss payee.

8.4 Taxes: Borrower shall pay all taxes when due unless contested in good faith.

8.5 Corporate Existence: Borrower shall maintain its corporate existence and good standing in all jurisdictions where it conducts business.

ARTICLE 9: NEGATIVE COVENANTS

9.1 Indebtedness: Borrower shall not incur additional Indebtedness exceeding $5,000,000 in the aggregate without Lender's prior written consent.

9.2 Liens: Borrower shall not create or permit any Lien on its assets except for Permitted Liens as defined herein.

9.3 Investments: Borrower shall not make Investments exceeding $2,000,000 in any fiscal year.

9.4 Dividends: Borrower shall not declare or pay dividends or make distributions to shareholders if an Event of Default exists or would result therefrom.

9.5 Asset Sales: Borrower shall not sell, transfer, or dispose of assets exceeding $3,000,000 in any fiscal year without Lender's consent.

9.6 Mergers and Acquisitions: Borrower shall not merge with or acquire any other entity without Lender's prior written consent.

ARTICLE 10: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

10.1 Organization: Borrower is duly organized, validly existing, and in good standing under the laws of Delaware.

10.2 Authority: Borrower has full power and authority to execute and deliver this Agreement and perform its obligations hereunder.

10.3 Financial Condition: The financial statements delivered to Lender fairly present Borrower's financial condition as of their respective dates.

10.4 Litigation: There is no pending or threatened litigation that could materially adversely affect Borrower's business or financial condition.

10.5 Compliance with Laws: Borrower is in compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and governmental requirements.

10.6 No Default: Borrower is not in default under any material agreement or obligation.

10.7 Taxes: All tax returns have been filed and all taxes have been paid when due.

ARTICLE 11: CONDITIONS PRECEDENT

11.1 Conditions to Closing: Lender's obligation to fund the Loan is subject to: (a) execution of this Agreement, (b) delivery of legal opinions, (c) receipt of corporate resolutions, (d) execution of Security Agreement, (e) perfection of security interests, (f) receipt of insurance certificates, and (g) payment of fees.

11.2 Representations True: All representations and warranties must be true and correct as of the Closing Date.

11.3 No Default: No Event of Default shall have occurred and be continuing.

ARTICLE 12: EVENTS OF DEFAULT

12.1 Payment Default: Failure to pay principal or interest within 5 business days of the due date.

12.2 Covenant Default: Failure to comply with any covenant, which failure continues for 30 days after notice from Lender.

12.3 Representation Default: Any representation or warranty proves to be materially false or misleading.

12.4 Cross-Default: Default under any other agreement involving Indebtedness exceeding $1,000,000.

12.5 Bankruptcy: Borrower commences bankruptcy proceedings or becomes subject to involuntary bankruptcy proceedings.

12.6 Judgment: Entry of a final judgment against Borrower exceeding $2,000,000 that remains unsatisfied for 30 days.

12.7 Material Adverse Change: Occurrence of a Material Adverse Change in Borrower's business, operations, or financial condition.

12.8 Change of Control: Any change in the ownership or control of Borrower without Lender's prior written consent.

ARTICLE 13: REMEDIES

13.1 Acceleration: Upon an Event of Default, Lender may declare all outstanding amounts immediately due and payable.

13.2 Interest Rate Increase: The Interest Rate shall automatically increase by 2.00% per annum upon an Event of Default.

13.3 Enforcement of Security: Lender may exercise all rights and remedies under the Security Agreement and applicable law.

13.4 Collection Costs: Borrower shall reimburse Lender for all costs of collection, including reasonable attorneys' fees.

ARTICLE 14: SECURITY AND COLLATERAL

14.1 Grant of Security Interest: Borrower grants Lender a first-priority security interest in all assets, including: (a) accounts receivable, (b) inventory, (c) equipment, (d) intellectual property, (e) deposit accounts, and (f) general intangibles.

14.2 Perfection: Borrower shall execute all documents necessary to perfect Lender's security interest, including UCC-1 financing statements.

14.3 Collateral Value: The aggregate value of the Collateral shall at all times equal or exceed 150% of the outstanding Loan balance.

ARTICLE 15: INDEMNIFICATION

15.1 General Indemnity: Borrower shall indemnify and hold harmless Lender from any losses, claims, damages, or expenses arising from this Agreement or the Loan.

15.2 Environmental Indemnity: Borrower shall indemnify Lender for any environmental liabilities related to Borrower's properties or operations.

15.3 Survival: The indemnification obligations shall survive repayment of the Loan and termination of this Agreement.

ARTICLE 16: GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

16.1 Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

16.2 Jurisdiction: Borrower consents to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in New York County, New York.

16.3 Waiver of Jury Trial: Both parties waive their right to a jury trial in any action arising from this Agreement.

ARTICLE 17: NOTICES

17.1 Method of Notice: All notices shall be in writing and delivered by: (a) personal delivery, (b) overnight courier, (c) certified mail, or (d) email with confirmation.

17.2 Addresses: Notices to Borrower shall be sent to the address set forth in Article 1. Notices to Lender shall be sent to the address set forth in Article 1.

17.3 Effectiveness: Notices shall be deemed effective upon receipt.

ARTICLE 18: AMENDMENTS AND WAIVERS

18.1 Written Amendments: This Agreement may be amended only by written agreement signed by both parties.

18.2 No Waiver: No waiver of any provision shall be effective unless in writing and signed by the party against whom enforcement is sought.

18.3 Continuing Effect: No waiver shall constitute a continuing waiver or waiver of any other provision.

ARTICLE 19: MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

19.1 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements.

19.2 Severability: If any provision is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

19.3 Successors and Assigns: This Agreement shall bind and benefit the parties and their successors and assigns.

19.4 Assignment: Borrower may not assign this Agreement without Lender's prior written consent. Lender may assign its rights without Borrower's consent.

19.5 Counterparts: This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be an original.

19.6 Expenses: Borrower shall reimburse Lender for all reasonable expenses incurred in connection with this Agreement, including legal fees.
